My Cilantro Rice Recipe

The Best Way to Prepare Rice

Rice can go with almost everything and this recipe mixes perfectly with the other components.
You could use white or brown rice but this recipe calls for white rice because it hits the craving.
You will need a rice cooker for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups White rice
  • 3 cups Water
  • 2 1/2 tbsp Finely chopped cilantro
  • 2 tbsp Unsalted butter
  • 1 Lime

Steps

  1. Pour 3 cups white rice into the rice pot.
  2. Wash the rice.
    3.
  3. Add 3 cups water to the rice and turn on the rice cooker.
  4. Finely chop the cilantro.
  5. When the rice is done, add cilantro, butter, zest from the lime, and the lime's juice.
  6. Mix and you're done.
