My Black Beans Recipe

Any Black Beans Will Do

It's not pretty but we're gonna use a can of black beans today for my recipe. We're just
gonna throw a bunch of ingredients in a pot to make a salsa.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Vegetable oil
  • 1 White onion
  • 1 Serrano chili
  • 1 Roma tomato
  • 1 can Black beans
  • 1 cup Chicken stock
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Using a medium size pot, set over medium heat, add in vegetable oil.
  2. Dice the onion and add to the pot. Salt to taste.
  3. Thinly slice the serrano pepper and rough chop the roma tomato. Add to the pot.
  4. Saute for 2-3 minutes or until onino begins to soften.
  5. Add the can of black beans and cup of chicken stock. Add salt.
  6. Bring to low heat and let simmer until vegetables soften.
  7. Turn off the heat. Stir in 4 cloves of finely chopped garlic.
