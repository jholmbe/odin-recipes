My Pickled Red Onions Recipe

"I love pickled foods"

Basically all that needs to be done is boil some salty vinegar water and let the red onions sit in it.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Red onion
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 cup White distilled vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Kosher salt

Steps

  1. Cut thin slices of the red onion and place in heat proof jar.
  2. In a saucepan, add 1 cup of water, 1 cup of white distilled vinegar, and 1 tbsp of kosher salt.
  3. Place it on medium-high heat and bring it to a boil.
  4. Immediately pour the boiling water over the red onions, completely submerging them.
  5. Let it sit at room temp until completely cooled.
