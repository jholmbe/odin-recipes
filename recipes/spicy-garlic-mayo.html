My Spicy Garlic Mayo Recipe

Banger recipe for all types of food

This recipes is simple with few ingredients for an efficient punch of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Mayo
  • 1/2 tbsp Onion powder
  • 8 cloves Garlic
  • 3.5 tbsp Hot sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Grate the garlic and add everything to the bowl. Stir and done.
