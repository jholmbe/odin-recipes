My Pollo Asado Recipe

14 chickens turned into Pollo Asado

This recipe is inspired by Mexican cuisine. Make sure to make a big batch of this stuff because you'll eat
right through it.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Chicken breast
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1/4 Red onion
  • 1/2 Juice from a orange
  • 1/2 Juice from a lime
  • 1 Habanero
  • 30g Achiote Paste
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. In a blender, add all ingredients (minus the chicken) to make a marinade.
  2. With the marinade, save 1/4th of it and use the rest on lathering the chicken breast.
  3. Marinade for at least 30 minutes and up to 4hrs.
  4. Add a thin layer of oil to the skillet. Once it is ripping hot, add the chicken.
  5. Sear on all sides, letting it burn and char.
  6. For perfectly cooking the chicken, let the internal temperature hover around 165F until cooked.
  7. Take the chicken off the skillet, cut into bite sized pieces.
  8. Lather the rest of the marinade over the chicken bites.
